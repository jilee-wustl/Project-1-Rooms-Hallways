Project Description - Rooms & Hallways

Although it is now ubiquitous, the hyperlink is the most radical feature of the internet. You will consider how to use the hyperlink for creative ends. The ability to hyperlink web pages together allows for all kinds of complex new readings. Select a visual composition or a text. Determine a way to break your composition down into meaningful 'rooms' (paragraphs, sentences, words or blocks of imagery, etc...). Now weave these distinct units together using many html pages and hyperlinks 'hallways' to create a network. Consider allowing for user choice, branching structures, loops, etc. The linking does not have to follow a linear sequence. See if you can produce new readings and new ways of traversing your composition.



Project for 'Interaction: Non-Linear Narrative' course at Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts, Washington Univ. in St. Louis, Spring 2018
